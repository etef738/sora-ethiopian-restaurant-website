Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take reservations?

+

Yes, we accept reservations for parties of any size. You can call us at (416) 555-1234 or use our online booking system. Walk-ins are also welcome based on availability.

Is parking available?

+

Free street parking is available on Danforth Avenue and surrounding streets. We're also a 5-minute walk from Donlands subway station.

Do you offer delivery and takeout?

+

Yes, we offer both delivery and takeout services. You can order directly through our website or call us. We also partner with major delivery apps for your convenience.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

+

Absolutely! We offer vegan, vegetarian, halal, and gluten-free options. Please inform your server about any allergies or dietary requirements, and we'll be happy to accommodate.

Do you have a minimum for catering orders?

+

Our minimum catering order is for 10 people. We require at least 48 hours notice for catering orders. For large events (50+ people), please give us at least one week's notice.
