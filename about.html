Our Story

A Journey from Ethiopia to Toronto

Founded in 1999, Sora Ethiopian Restaurant began as a dream of Mama Sora, who brought her grandmother's recipes from the highlands of Ethiopia to the vibrant streets of Toronto's Danforth Avenue.

For over 25 years, we have been serving authentic Ethiopian cuisine using traditional cooking methods, organic teff flour, and indigenous spices imported directly from Ethiopia. Our commitment to authenticity and quality has made us a beloved destination for both the Ethiopian community and food enthusiasts across Toronto.

Today, Sora is more than just a restaurant ‚Äì it's a cultural hub where people gather to enjoy great food, watch sports, experience live Ethiopian music, and celebrate life's special moments.
