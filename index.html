Why Choose Sora Ethiopian Restaurant in East York Toronto?

100% Organic Teff Injera

Authentic teff injera made from premium organic teff grain, naturally gluten-free and rich in nutrients. Our traditional fermentation process creates the perfect spongy texture.

Indigenous Ethiopian Ingredients

We import authentic spices and ingredients directly from Ethiopia, including berbere, mitmita, and other traditional seasonings for genuine flavors.

Extensive Vegan & Fasting Options

Traditional Ethiopian fasting dishes (beyayenetu) with fresh vegetables, lentils, and legumes. Perfect for vegan, vegetarian, and health-conscious diners.

Non-GMO & Healthy Options

All our teff products and ingredients are non-GMO, organic, and naturally healthy. We prioritize clean eating and traditional preparation methods.

Prime Danforth Area Location

Conveniently located in East York Toronto's vibrant Danforth area, easily accessible by TTC and offering a warm, authentic Ethiopian dining atmosphere.

Traditional Coffee Ceremony

Experience authentic Ethiopian coffee ceremony with fresh roasted beans. Available for special events, weddings, and cultural celebrations.
